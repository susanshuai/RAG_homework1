### Here are some tips to help you become a book lover:

1. **Start Small**:
   - Choose shorter books or novellas to build your confidence.
   - Consider graphic novels or illustrated books that are visually engaging.

2. **Find Your Genre**:
   - Explore different genres (mystery, fantasy, romance, etc.) to discover what interests you most.
   - Look for popular recommendations in those genres.

3. **Set a Reading Routine**:
   - Dedicate a specific time each day for reading, even if it’s just 10-15 minutes.
   - Create a cozy reading environment to make it enjoyable.

4. **Join a Book Club**:
   - Engage with others who share a passion for reading.
   - Discussing books can enhance your understanding and enjoyment.

5. **Use Audiobooks**:
   - Listen to audiobooks during your commute or while doing chores.
   - This can help you get used to storytelling without the pressure of reading.

6. **Explore Book Adaptations**:
   - Watch movies or shows based on books and then read the original stories.
   - This can pique your interest in the source material.

7. **Visit Libraries or Bookstores**:
   - Spend time browsing and discovering

new titles that catch your eye.
   - Libraries often have reading programs and events that can make reading more fun.

8. **Read Reviews and Recommendations**:
   - Follow book blogs, YouTube channels, or social media accounts that focus on book reviews.
   - Hearing others' excitement about books can inspire you to pick one up.

9. **Set Reading Goals**:
   - Challenge yourself to read a certain number of books each month or year.
   - Track your progress and celebrate your achievements.

10. **Be Patient**:
    - It’s okay if you don’t love every book you pick up. Keep exploring until you find ones that resonate with you.
    - Reading should be enjoyable, so don’t feel pressured to finish books that don’t engage you.
